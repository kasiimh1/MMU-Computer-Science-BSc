About Myself

My Name is Kasim Hussain I am currently 21 years of age. I'm also in my first year studying Computer Science BSc at Manchester Metropolitan University (MMU). I moved from Birmingham to follow my dreams to become a Software Engineer. My road towards my goal hasn't been easy but like Steve Jobs said "Stay hungry. Stay foolish."

Where Steve attended Stanford University in 2005 to give that speech to a bunch of students. "You've got to find what you love." Another iconic quote from the late Steve Jobs that he said to the students who attended the speech, to this day I stand by it, this is the reason why I am studying Computer Science because I love everything it's about.



One of the main reasons why I chose to study Computer Science is because from a young age I had always been interested in the Computer Technology Digital space and keeping up to date in it. That wasn't enough for me though, I wanted to be one of the people that can push the hardware or software which will power the future devices in the years to come.

I had always wanted to learn to code but I never had a starting point to my hardware orientated background. Picking Computer Science allows me to study something I have a passion for, giving me a start to the beginning of my journey to get where I want to be.
