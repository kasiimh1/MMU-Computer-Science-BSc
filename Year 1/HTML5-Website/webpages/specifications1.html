Course Specification 2

Below contains a brief explanation of the units that are studied in year 1 when studying Computer Science at Manchester Metropolitan University.

Computer Science BSc in Year 1

  1. Computer System Fundamentals

Containing the fundamental principles like, Digital Logic, Assembly Language Programming, Function, Sets and Logic and finally Discrete Mathematics. Which will come in use over the period of the degree.

  1. Information Systems

Allows building skills, such as communication and working within a team. We are also introduced to how Information Systems is used within organisations (or how it replaces legacy practices).
